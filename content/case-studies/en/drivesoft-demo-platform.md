The scope was intentionally focused: build a demo environment that feels production-grade without production integration complexity.

## Delivered capabilities

- Search and booking journey with realistic states and edge cases.
- Reusable UI kit for fast scenario adaptation.
- Localization-ready architecture for multilingual presentations.
- Structured flow that keeps sales and product teams aligned.

## Strategic value

The demo platform became a practical pre-sales asset, improving confidence in both technical and business conversations.
